Return & Refund Policy

At Aditya's Pharma, we strive to ensure that you are satisfied with every purchase. However, if you are not completely happy with your order, we offer a simple and transparent return process. Please read the following policy carefully:

1. Prescription Medications

  • Non-returnable: Due to safety and hygiene reasons, prescription medications cannot be returned or exchanged unless they are defective or damaged during delivery.
  • Incorrect or Damaged Orders: If you received the wrong prescription or damaged medication, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order. We will arrange for a return or replacement free of charge.
  • Refund Process: For defective or incorrect prescriptions, we will process a full refund or send a replacement depending on your preference.

2. Over-the-Counter (OTC) Medications

  • Returnable within 30 Days: OTC medications can be returned within 30 days from the date of purchase, as long as the product is unopened and in its original packaging.
  • Conditions for Return: The product must be unused, with the seal intact, and in resellable condition.
  • Refund Process: Once we receive the returned item, we will issue a refund to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

3. Personal Care & Beauty Products

  • Returnable within 14 Days: Personal care, beauty, and hygiene products can be returned within 14 days if they are unopened and unused.
  • Exceptions: Due to hygiene and safety concerns, opened personal care products, including skincare and makeup, are non-returnable.

4. Health Devices & Medical Equipment

  • Returnable within 30 Days: Unopened health devices and medical equipment can be returned within 30 days.
  • Defective Items: If the item is defective or malfunctions, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order for a replacement or full refund.
  • Return Conditions: The product must be in its original, unused condition with all accessories, manuals, and packaging.

5. Baby & Childcare Products

  • Returnable within 30 Days: Unopened and unused baby products can be returned within 30 days from the date of purchase.
  • Exceptions: Diapers, formula, and food items are non-returnable due to safety and hygiene standards.

6. Sale & Discounted Items

  • Non-returnable: Items purchased on sale, clearance, or with special discounts are non-returnable unless they are defective or damaged.

How to Return an Item:

  1. Contact Us: Please reach out to our customer service team at info@adityaspharma.com or call +91 7720053626 with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Return Authorization: We will provide you with a return authorization and instructions for the return process.
  3. Packaging: Please package the item securely and include your order number and return form in the package.
  4. Shipping: You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong or defective item).
  5. Refund: Once the return is processed, we will issue a refund to your original payment method. Please allow 7-10 business days for the refund to appear on your account.

Exchanges

We do not offer exchanges for products. If you need a different item, please initiate a return for the original product and place a new order for the desired item.

Important Notes:

  • Prescription medication returns are only accepted if there was a mistake made in the order or the item was damaged during shipping.
  • All returns must include proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation).
  • We reserve the right to refuse returns if the product does not meet the above criteria.
